    Hell

    hell (lake of fire) (gheh'-en-nah)

    ```col-md
### Definition
valley of (the son of) Hinnom; ge-henna (or Ge-Hinnom), a valley of Jerusalem, used (figuratively) as a name for the place (or state) of everlasting punishment:—hell.
```
