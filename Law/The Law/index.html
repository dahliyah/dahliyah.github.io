    Some translations say lawlessness instead of iniquity.

    • [n] My Comments: Faith without works is dead. Works meaning action and character. If your fruits and character do not bear the fruit of Yah, then you are working iniquity, lawlessness. How do we bear the fruit of Yah? We do his commands, these are the instructions of righteousness. Faith in Yah and Yahusha is shown by your actions. Faith does not only mean believing that "Jesus died for you on the cross" which is not the full story I might add, but I digress, it means that you must align your character in the same way that Yah walked. And again how did he walk? Who is Yahusha (Jesus) > His Walk➕ 2023-07-21
