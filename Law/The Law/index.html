    The Law

    Due to the writings of Paul and the way they are worded, Christians believe that the word "law" is ALWAYS referring to the laws in the old testament. Because of this many believe that the laws mentioned in the old testament is a "curse". That following the law was a curse imposed by God and that we are under a blessing now. This is not entirely the case.
