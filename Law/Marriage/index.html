    Marriage

    “Chained” Women, or Agunot

    Agunot are women trapped in marriages because they cannot receive a rabbinic divorce. It's due to their husbands or their husband's male next of kin being unable or unwilling to provide one. The term "agunot" comes from the Hebrew word for "anchor."

    In Israel, some men choose to run away to avoid taking responsibility for their families since getting a divorce is quite difficult there.

    This does not align with the teachings of the Torah (Yahuah).
